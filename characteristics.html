Characteristics

CHARACTERISTICS OF SNOWSHOE CATS

Rounded Head

Medium-sized cat with rounded head, almost as wide as it is long. Cheekbones are set high with a firm chin and a good length muzzle.

Eyes & Ears

Size medium to medium large, in proportion to body with a broad base, slightly rounded ear tips. Oval shaped eyes, with greater length than depth; slanted toward base of ears, one eye width apart; bright, sparking, expressive. Color blue.

The Glorious Body

Long and firm medium-sized body with medium musculature, powerful but not bulky. Medium tail with slight tapering to end, length in proportion to body. It combines the heftiness of American Shorthair with the length of Siamese.

Key Characteristics

in Snowshoe Cats

Facial Features

Their heads can be either triangular or apple-shaped, and many of these beauties have markings on their faces, with an upside down "V" marking that is one of the standards for the breed.

Snow Shoes

Paws are white, giving the breed the look from which their name was derived. The white can extend up the legs to varying degrees.

Coating

The coat is short, and combines the pointed colouration with tuxedo markings and as with all pointed coloured cats, the kittens are born white with the points darkening as they age.

Personality

Snowshoes are generally affectionate, sweet-tempered, and mellow. Snowshoes are very social and docile, and show great devotion and love towards their owners.
