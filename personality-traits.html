Personality & Traits

Love & Affection

Snowshoes are generally affectionate. They enjoy the company of humans and being given attention, and are compatible with children and other pets. Snowshoes are very social and docile, and show great devotion and love towards their owners. They love to touch and be touched. Very intelligent, they can be taught a number of tricks.

Goofy & Chatty

They are inclined to be inquisitive and curious, sometimes to the point of danger, though typically clever enough to get themselves out of trouble. Some are more friendly, some are loners, some take on a rather ‘care-giver’ role with their people but they are certainly not a boring cat.
